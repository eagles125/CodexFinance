# Policy And News Driven Screening

## Method

1. Define the event: policy, regulation, fiscal support, geopolitical event, technology breakthrough, commodity shock, or industry news.
2. Map the transmission chain: who receives revenue, margin, volume, valuation, or funding benefit.
3. Separate beneficiaries:
   - Direct: listed company sells the product/service explicitly affected.
   - Indirect: upstream/downstream or substitutable exposure.
   - Sentiment: popular narrative with weak earnings link.
4. Score evidence:
   - 3: revenue/profit exposure visible in filings or segment data.
   - 2: credible exposure but incomplete quantitative proof.
   - 1: narrative-only or peer association.
5. Remove obvious traps: tiny liquidity, repeated failed pivots, regulatory flags, excessive valuation, weak balance sheet, or event already fully priced.
6. Produce watchlist, not buy list.

## Output Fields

- Ticker
- Company
- Market
- Theme
- Exposure logic
- Evidence score
- Catalyst date
- Valuation/liquidity note
- Key risk
- Verification needed
