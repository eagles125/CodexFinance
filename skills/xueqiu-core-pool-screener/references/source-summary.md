1. Use hard factors to create a smaller base pool.
2. Use AI-assisted deeper scoring on that base pool.

- Profit growth: at least two years of net profit growth above 10%.

The AI-assisted analysis then considers:

- Rough DCF valuation.
- Free cash flow.
- Announcement summaries.
- AI analysis of company quality and recent information.
